about

We are "Tsukuba Fireworks Producers",
an official club in Univ. of Tsukuba.

Our actions:

・produce fireworks of the school festival
・go to see fireworks

fireworks

We recommend some of fireworks in Japan.
You must go there and watch them with your own eyes!

  • Univalve Festival @Niigata

    Niigata is famous for fireworks.
    "four syaku dama" is the biggest fireworks in the world.
    It's just overwhelming...!

  • Nagano Ebisuko Fireworks Festival @Nagano

    "Music star mine",
    which is the fusion of music and fireworks,
    　will give you full impact.

  • Joso Kinugawa Fireworks Festival @Ibaraki

    You can watch fireworks which are made
    by some of the best fireworks technicians
    and have extremely high quality.
